About Me

I'm Heriberto Beard from Torrington, Connecticut. With over five years in the CRM and email communication arena, my passion thrives at the crossroads of cutting-edge email design and robust customer relationship management. My credentials include being a Salesforce Marketing Cloud Email Specialist and holding a Google Data Analytics Certificate. Outside the professional realm, I'm dedicated to fostering a culture of continuous learning and innovation, a commitment evident during my time at Blue State, where I championed interactive email design sessions.

What I do

Equipped with proficiency in HTML/CSS3, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, and interactive email designs, I excel in creating emails that not only bypass spam filters but also ensure an exceptional user experience across devices.
